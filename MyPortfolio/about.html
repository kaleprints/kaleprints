About Me

Hello I'm Kalli! I'm 17 years old and my shop is based in California. I started Kale Prints to share my art and as a side gig that has flexible hours for my busy academic schedule. Art and creating are two things that I'm incredibly passionate about, and I hope that these prints can add some more joy into your life!

Kale Prints is perfect for you if you're looking for colorful and imaginative pieces to use as room decor, journaling, or as a gift for a friend! Each print is made of cardstock and is perfect to write letters on or to scrapbook.

Although this shop is brand new, one of my goals is to watch it grow and create many more different prints that are unique to me and my style.

A few of my hobbies that I like to do in my free time are painting, baking, and studying French and Chinese. I'm currently a student attending high school but after I graduate I would love to major in either graphic design or computer science. But ideally I would love to spend all day in a little art studio painting and illustrating in France.

Feel free to take a look around my site and Instagram! Make sure you come back once in a while, because I'm always illustrating and will have some more prints ready to be purchased!
